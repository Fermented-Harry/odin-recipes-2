Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken Katsu is an easy dish that takes very little time to make, and it's also very delicious! Following is how you can make these treats for yourself at home.

Ingredients

  • chicken breats
  • breadcrumbs
  • flour
  • salt
  • spices of your choice
  • frying oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  • First, marinate your chicken breasts in the spices of your choice, I usually use salt, paprika, sugar and lime juice.
  • Marinate your chicken breasts for half an hour, 1 hour, or even overnight. Your decision>
  • Get three plates and put the flour, eggs and breadcrumbs in each plate.
  • Take your chicken and dip it in the eggs first. Make sure you get every part of the chicken covered in egg.
  • Then place it in the flour and cover every part of it too. When you're done with that, repeat again but in the breadcrumbs.
  • Place it aside and continue with the rest of the chicken
  • Fry in oil at medium heat for about 3 minutes each side, or until golden brown.

    • And there you have it! Beautifully gold, crispy and tender chicken breasts.
