Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a cheap and easy meal, that takes so little time. It is absolutely perfect for college students and I would recommend that you try it right now!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • eggs
  • vegetables of your choice
  • salt
  • garlic
  • pepper
  • onions
  • water
  • oil
  • meat of your choice

Steps

  1. First of all, we have to cook our rice. Old rice from last night works best because it has become stale and can soak more moisture and flavor. However, if you're gonna cook the rice now, it is perfectly fine too.
  2. While your rice is cooking, put some oil in a pan or wok. When the oil is hot, place in your diced onions and garlics. Put your meat when the onions and garlics are soft and stir fry till cooked.
  3. Put in your vegetables next.
  4. When they are all finally cooked, put your rice in and stir well. A bit after putting rice, crack an egg or two in the pan.
  5. Mix well.

Enjoy this tasty meal while it's still hot!
