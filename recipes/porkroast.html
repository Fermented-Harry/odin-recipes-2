Pork belly roast

Description

Pork belly roast is one of the best dishes to eat, especially of you like crispyness, but also if you like soft and tender meat. It is also so easy to make, and you dont even need to be watching it!

Ingredients

  • Pork belly roast
  • salt
  • sugar
  • paprika
  • some oil
  • potatoes or bread(anything works, we just something to soak the pork fat that will drip down)

Steps

  1. Cut diagonal slits into the skin of the pork belly roast. It should look like a checkerboard.
  2. Rub your salt into the skinside of the pork. Make sure to really rub in carefully between each slit.
  3. Now on the meat-side, rub in your salt, sugar and paprika. Let this sit for a few minutes or overnight.
  4. Preheat your oven to 160 degrees Celcius.
  5. When you're ready to bake, place your pork belly on a grill, and your sliced potatoes on a baking tray.
  6. Season the potatoes with some salt and some oil; just enough to not stick the potatoes.
  7. Place everthing in the oven for around 30 minutes. You might want to flip the potatoes around 10 minutes to even the cooking.

Eat this delicious meal with some sauce! It has crispy skin with a soft, melty fat, and tender meat!
